Disease Class: Bacterial Leaf Streak
==================================================

Description: Bacterial disease causing streaks

Symptoms: Yellow to brown streaks on leaves

Treatment: Copper compounds, field sanitation

Image Requirements:
- Add 50-200 images per class for good training
- Use JPG, PNG formats
- Clear, well-lit images preferred
- Various angles and conditions
- Image size: 224x224 pixels or larger

Sources for images:
- Plant pathology databases
- Agricultural research institutes
