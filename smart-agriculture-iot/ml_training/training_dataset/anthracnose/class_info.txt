Disease Class: Anthracnose
==================================================

Description: Fungal disease causing dark lesions

Symptoms: Dark, sunken spots, pink spore masses

Treatment: Fungicide spray, remove plant debris

Image Requirements:
- Add 50-200 images per class for good training
- Use JPG, PNG formats
- Clear, well-lit images preferred
- Various angles and conditions
- Image size: 224x224 pixels or larger

Sources for images:
- Plant pathology databases
- Agricultural research institutes
- Field photographs
- Online plant disease resources
