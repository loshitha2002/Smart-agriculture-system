Plant Disease Dataset Training Guide
========================================

1. ADDING IMAGES:
   - Add 50-200 images per disease class
   - Use clear, high-quality images
   - Ensure proper lighting and focus

2. IMAGE SOURCES:
   - PlantNet (https://plantnet.org/)
   - Plant Pathology databases
   - Agricultural university resources
   - Field photography

3. TRAINING COMMAND:
   python train_disease_model.py --data_dir training_dataset

4. EXPECTED RESULTS:
   - Training time: 1-3 hours
   - Expected accuracy: 85-95%
   - Model size: ~50MB
